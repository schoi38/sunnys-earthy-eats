Brussels Sprouts

Description

I'll teach you how to make the best damn charred brussels sprouts you've ever had.

You ready?

Ingredients

  • Brussels Sprouts
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Sea Salt
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Cut brussels sprouts into manageable bites and put into glass baking pan.
  2. Mince garlic and distribute over brussels sprouts.
  3. Pour a load of parmesan cheese over brussels sprouts.
  4. Add salt.
  5. Mix together.
  6. Roast alongside the salmon for 2o-25 minutes.
