Sweet Potatoes

Description

SImple. Easy. Tasty.

Get ready.

Ingredients

  • Sweet Potatoes

Steps

  1. Tear off aluminum foil to size properly with convection oven.
  2. Wash sweet potatoes.
  3. Place them onto the foil and into the convection oven with ample space in between.
  4. Turn 400 degrees and time out 46 minutes.
  5. Poke a chopstick or something similar in to test readiness. It should be soft and easy to poke all the way through.
